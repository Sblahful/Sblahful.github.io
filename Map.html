How to get to Hawick Children's Farm

We're just a 2 minute drive off the A4 between Carlisle and Edinburgh.

To find us, take a turning south of Hawick village up Wilton Hill Lane. We are the first farmhouse on the right, and well signposted around the area.

If these directions are a touch aesoteric for you, you can always let Google show you how to reach us.
