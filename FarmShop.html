Hawick Farm Shop

Quite possibly the finest fruit & veg in Scotland

Here at Hawick Farm we've provided food for the nation for generations, and now at our award-winning shop you can get the finest produce from around the country. Our new organic range is pretty good too.

Our farm shop is situated in the beautiful converted barn at Hawick Children's Farm. At our butchers you can order sausages, bacon, joints, steaks, offal, and fine tripe.
