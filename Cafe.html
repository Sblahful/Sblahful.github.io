The Hawick Farm Cafe

Rest & be thankful with fresh cakes & coffee

Whether you're after a hearty lunch before a long hike, or are just looking for a wee respite from a lengthy drive, you'll be glad you chose Hawick Farm Cafe. We offer homemade hot lunches and a fine selection of sandwiches proudly made from local produce. Our vegan and vegetarian options are especially popular.

Those with a sweet tooth will love our freshly baked cakes. The selection varies weekly and takes full advantage of seasonal local fruit.
