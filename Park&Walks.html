PARK & WALKS

Hawick has some of the best walks in Scotland on its doorstep. Whether you're looking for a relaxing stroll around our parkland, or a full-day hike exploring the Scottish Borders, let us be your guide.

PARKS & WALKS

Explore the best of the borders

Hawick Farm sits in some of the finest walking country the Scottish borders has to offer. Whether you're looking for a challenging hike or just a stroll to stretch your legs after a long drive, there's plenty of options for all.

We have hundreds of miles of walks in the area, and Ordnance Survey maps for sale in our shop. If you can't decide where to go we'll be happy to offer some advice.
